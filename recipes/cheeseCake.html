Cheese Cake

Cheesecake is a rich, creamy dessert made mainly from cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, usually on a crust of crushed cookies or graham crackers. It can be baked or unbaked and is often chilled before serving, known for its smooth texture and decadent flavor.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Butter
  • Graham crackers

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C) and grease a springform pan.
  2. Crush the graham crackers and mix with melted butter to form the crust.
  3. Press the crust into the bottom of the pan and bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Stir in the sour cream and vanilla extract until fully combined.
  7. Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top.
  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the center is set but slightly wobbly.
  9. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.
  10. Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving. Enjoy!
